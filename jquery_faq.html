Starter Pokemon:

Charmander
Charmander (Japanese: ヒトカゲ Hitokage) is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Charmeleon starting at level 16, which evolves into Charizard starting at level 36.
Bulbasaur
Bulbasaur (Japanese: フシギダネ Fushigidane) is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16, which evolves into Venusaur starting at level 32.
Squirtle
Squirtle (Japanese: ゼニガメ Zenigame) is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Wartortle starting at level 16, which evolves into Blastoise starting at level 36.
Cyndaquil
Cyndaquil (Japanese: ヒノアラシ Hinoarashi) is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It evolves into Quilava starting at level 14, which evolves into Typhlosion starting at level 36.
Chikorita
Chikorita (Japanese: チコリータ Chicorita) is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It evolves into Bayleef starting at level 16, which evolves into Meganium starting at level 32.
Totodile
Totodile (Japanese: ワニノコ Waninoko) is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It evolves into Croconaw starting at level 18, which evolves into Feraligatr starting at level 30.
Torchic
Torchic (Japanese: アチャモ Achamo) is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves into Combusken starting at level 16, which evolves into Blaziken starting at level 36
Treecko
Treecko (Japanese: キモリ Kimori) is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves into Grovyle starting at level 16, which evolves into Sceptile starting at level 36.
Mudkip
Mudkip (Japanese: ミズゴロウ Mizugorou) is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves into Marshtomp starting at level 16, which evolves into Swampert starting at level 36.
